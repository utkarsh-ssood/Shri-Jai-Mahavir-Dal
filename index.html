Welcome to our site

Shri Jai Mahavir Sewa Samiti Dal is a devoted religious organization with a rich history of 25 years dedicated to promoting the spiritual teachings of Lord Hanuman. Our organization takes immense pride in organizing a grand annual event that spans three sacred days: Ashtami, Navami, and Dussehra. These days are marked with fervent devotion, cultural festivities, and the reenactment of Lord Hanuman's divine role.

You can find us at our sacred temple, "Panj Pipli Mandir," located in Bahadurpur, Hoshiarpur. Our temple is a place of solace and spiritual enlightenment, where devotees come to seek the blessings and guidance of Lord Hanuman.
